Net change

Window:
Per-person change over the selected window from transitions only — clients moving between team members. Routine hours and closes move with the client, so everything balances to zero across the team. The quarterly bump row (when shown) reports extra workload from quarterly clients hitting their trigger month inside the window.

Scheduled changes

Tag key: (Head) — Rachel Armentor / Kelley Martin, leadership members whose direct client engagements should ideally stay low. Open seat — placeholder slots (names prefixed with _) representing unassigned clients or clients scheduled to transition to a future hire.
Closes & hours logic: Monthly clients contribute every month. Quarterly clients only in Apr / Jul / Oct / Jan. Annual and One-time clients excluded from client counts and hours per project rules.
Actual hours caveat: Each client's actual average reflects historical pace. The green dots are "what we'd expect at current pace" applied to the projected monthly portfolio — useful for spotting capacity pressure, not a hard forecast.
Filter: applies to all charts and tables. Resets when switching tabs.
